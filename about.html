About Us

Shiva's Chilli Products is a producer and exporter of premium chilli powders and by-products. We blend traditional practices with modern hygiene and quality controls to deliver reliable, high-performing products for both retail and industrial use.

Our Mission

Deliver pure, chemical-free chilli products to domestic and international markets while supporting farmer livelihoods and sustainable sourcing.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a reliable, quality-first chilli supplier for retailers, restaurants, and food manufacturers worldwide.

Our Heritage

Shiva Associates has been operating since 1975. Over five decades we have grown from a local family-run operation into a trusted supplier for domestic and export markets — always guided by a customer-centric approach. We focus on consistent product quality, strong supplier relationships, and responsive service so our customers get reliable, high-quality chillies and by-products every time.
