Facilities

Our on-site facilities are purpose-built to ensure quality and traceability from farm to pack.

Sun-drying

Large open sun-drying yards where chillies are spread and turned to achieve optimal moisture levels. Our drying practices preserve colour and flavour while ensuring food safety.

Stemless Preparation Yards

Dedicated yards for stem removal and sorting with manual and mechanical processes to reduce contamination and leave only premium fruit for processing.

Dry-chilli Cleaning & Grinding Unit

A modern cleaning line removes foreign materials, followed by hygienic grinding lines with adjustable mesh sizes for different product grades.

Cold Storage Unit

Temperature-controlled storage for finished and semi-processed products to maintain freshness, quality, and compliance with export standards.
